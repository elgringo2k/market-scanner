Most Popular Premier League Betting Markets

When betting on the Premier League, there’s no shortage of exciting markets to explore. Whether you prefer simple match bets or season-long wagers, Oddschecker gives you access to all the most popular options — with the latest odds and offers from top UK bookmakers.

  1. Match Result: The most straightforward market, simply bet on the home win, draw, or away win. Perfect for casual punters and accumulators alike.
  2. Both Teams to Score (BTTS): A favourite among Premier League fans. You’re simply betting on whether both sides will find the net during the 90 minutes. This can also be added to an accumulator bet.
  3. Over/Under Goals: Predict whether the total number of goals in a match will be over or under a set line (e.g. 2.5 goals).
  4. Correct Score: A more challenging market, but with bigger odds. You’re predicting the exact final score of the match.
  5. First Goalscorer/Anytime Goalscorer: Back your favourite player to open the scoring or score at any point in the match. Popular for regular Premier League goal scorers like Erling Haaland or Mohamed Salah.
  6. Outright Markets: Season-long bets such as Premier League Winner, Top Four Finish, Relegation, or Top Goalscorer. These odds can shift quite dramatically throughout the season, with some value to be found at certain points.
  7. Accumulators (Accas): Combine multiple selections into one bet to boost your potential payout. Oddschecker makes it easy to build and compare your Premier League accas in seconds.
